My Favorite Place

Quebec City, Quebec

Admittedly, I have done little traveling in my life; however, my favorite place that I have gone thus far was Quebec City, Quebec. I went there over a year ago with one of my friends before we started college I have been other places in Canada, but something about Quebec City was so much more special to me.

Although Montreal had more things to do in a sense, Quebec City was so beautiful everywhere. I would highly recommend going and would love to make a trip back soon.
